Community Outreach Activities

7/12/2025
Tools 4 School 2025 — Community Outreach Activity

La Campesina participated in the 13th Annual Phoenix Tools 4 School event, a community initiative providing free backpacks and school supplies to students in grades K–8. Prior to the event, the station aired announcements inviting families and community members to attend and benefit from the available educational resources. During the event, La Campesina was present on-site, interacting with attendees, supporting community engagement efforts, and helping create a welcoming atmosphere for families. The event connected local students with essential school supplies while promoting educational readiness and community involvement among Phoenix-area families.

7/12/2025
CASA Academy Back-to-School Community Event

La Campesina participated in a back-to-school community event at CASA Academy, providing music, entertainment, and family engagement activities for students and parents. Station representatives interacted with attendees, hosted games and activities, and helped create a positive and welcoming atmosphere for local families. The event supported community involvement and celebrated students as they prepared for the new school year.

7/18/2025
Affordable Housing Community Outreach — Casa Bill Soltero

La Campesina participated in a community outreach event at Casa Bill Soltero Affordable Housing, engaging with senior residents through music, entertainment, and personal interaction. Radio hosts spent time with residents, providing a fun and positive atmosphere while encouraging social connection and community involvement. The event supported the well-being of local seniors by promoting engagement, companionship, and quality-of-life activities for residents of the affordable housing community.

7/26/2025
Stuff the Bus — Community Outreach Activity

La Campesina partnered with the City of Glendale for the Stuff the Bus community service event, helping support local students and families as they prepared for the upcoming school year. Through on-air announcements and community outreach efforts, the station encouraged listeners to donate school supplies and participate in the drive. During the event, station representatives interacted with community members while collecting backpacks and school supplies to help fill a school bus for students in need. Free refreshments were provided to attendees, and the event fostered community involvement, educational support, and collaboration among residents, organizations, and local leaders working to improve educational opportunities for children throughout the Glendale community.

9/10/2025
ASU Art Museum Community Outreach Activity

La Campesina supported community engagement with the ASU Art Museum by promoting the museum and its exhibitions through on-air announcements and live event participation. Station representatives spent two hours on-site encouraging community members and families to visit the museum, explore its galleries, and experience cultural and educational programming. Special emphasis was placed on inviting the public to view the exhibition “Tierras Reimaginadas: Migration,” featuring the artwork “Sangre De Nopal,” along with other museum galleries and exhibits.

La Campesina also supported the student organization M.E.Ch.A. (Movimiento Estudiantil Chicano de Aztán) by helping promote student involvement, leadership, and community engagement.
